Simple Summary: All Ryan Bergeron apps are designed with privacy in mind. Our iOS apps operate entirely on your device and do not collect or transmit any personal information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

All Ryan Bergeron iOS apps (including Daily Proverb, BrighterStart, and future apps) are designed to respect your privacy completely. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Any content you view or interact with in the app
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data

All app functionality operates entirely on your device. Your reading history, preferences, and any other app data remain private and local to your device.

2. How Our Apps Work

All Ryan Bergeron apps operate with privacy as a core principle:

  • All content and functionality is included with each app
  • Most features work completely offline after download
  • All data stays on your device
  • We cannot see what you read or how you use any of our apps
  • Each app operates independently without external data collection

3. In-App Purchases

Some of our apps may offer in-app purchases or premium features processed through Apple's App Store. Any payment information is handled exclusively by Apple and is subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not receive or store any payment or personal information from these transactions.

4. Website Data Collection

While our iOS apps collect no data, our website (brighterstart.app) may collect minimal information such as:

  • Basic web analytics (page views, general location)
  • Contact information if you voluntarily contact us

This website data is separate from and not connected to any of our iOS apps.

5. Third-Party Services

Our iOS apps do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. All our apps operate independently without external connections that could compromise your privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information from any of our apps, our apps are safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. No personal data is collected from users of any age across any of our applications.

7. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted from any of our apps, there is no personal data at risk. All app functionality operates locally on your device using iOS's built-in security features.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

If we ever change our app to collect data in the future, we will provide clear notice and obtain appropriate consent as required by law.
