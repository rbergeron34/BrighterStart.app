Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?

Tap the "Upgrade to Premium" button in the app to access additional Bible versions, save favorites, and use the spiritual journal feature.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings:
1. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions
2. Find "Daily Wisdom Premium"
3. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Can I restore my Premium features on a new device?

Yes! Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase Premium, and your subscription will automatically restore.

How do I save my favorite verses?

With Premium, tap the heart icon next to any verse to save it to your favorites list. Access your favorites from the main menu.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps:
1. Force close and restart the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest version from the App Store
4. If issues persist, contact us at the email above
